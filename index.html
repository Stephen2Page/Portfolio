About Me

Stephen has rediscovered his passion for writing software. The creation of programs of instructions for systems and testing and verification match with his drive for detail and clarity.

He has over 15 years finding solutions when computers and mobile devices are not working as expected. The process of replication, isolation and resolution benefits from his eye for detail. Working in a team environment required ongoing communication and feedback.

Previously he had 10 years in lighting design and theater technology. Producing art by committee requires strong teamwork and communication to meet the immovable deadline of opening night.

Stephen spends his spare time with his 12 year old rescued shepherd mix and weaving on one of his several looms. He has been involved with the Medina Spinning & Weaving Guild for eight years and has been producing their newsletter for most of that time and maintaining the website for the last five years.
